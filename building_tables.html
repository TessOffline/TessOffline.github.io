  • California had the highest voter turnout percentage and the largest number of voters among the three states.
  • Texas had the highest percentage of Republican support compared to the other states, despite having a lower turnout than California.
  • Florida had a voter turnout higher than Texas but lower than California, with a nearly even split in party support between Democrats and Republicans.
