LOCAL BIRD GOES WILD

Posted by Tess (THE COOL PERSON)

10th of June 2024

Local bird known as Wojtek went crazy when he flew down to the local mall and started attacking the shoppers. A person named Alexandra got pecked on the head while she was at the grocery store. "The bird was pecking me and grabbing me by the hair!" said Alexandra.

"I was screaming as I tried to run away from it and call animal control, I was scared for my life!"
